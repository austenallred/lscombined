Free Mini Bootcamp

A free introduction to web development (HTML, CSS and JavaScript) that also serves as Lambda School's pre-course work.

schedule

The mini bootcamp runs for four weeks, two days per week, from 6 p.m. to 9 p.m. Pacific time.

Time

Lectures are 6 p.m. to 9 p.m. Pacific and last about 45 minutes, two days per week. Expect 2-3 hours of homework per lecture.

Topics

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; a rapid introduction to web development, perfect for newcomers to programming.

Price

100% free. There is an optional $5 fee should a student want the certificate that comes for completing each homework assignment.
